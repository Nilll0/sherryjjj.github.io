I'm Elias, a designer and recovering developer based in Graz  (AT) always looking to make things useful.

About

My design work is focused on creating digital products that make brand interactions tangible, use technological possibilities and make sense to build. Exploring and defining a visual style is crucial to me in shaping a design that works, while also keeping information architecture, design systems and an inventive use of brand elements in  mind.

I either oversee special parts within a project or lead a project through from wireframes to development handoff. Both solo and within larger teams. In the past years I've worked for some exciting companies and  agencies.
